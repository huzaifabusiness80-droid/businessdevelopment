Business Management System (BMS)- Module
Specifications
This document outlines the features and functionality for the proposed Business
Management Application. In addition to your requested modules, I have added
HRM (Payroll) and CRM features to make it a complete ERP solution.
1. Company & Users (Admin Panel)
Purpose: Manage organizational details and user access security. * Company
Profile: Setup company name, logo, address, phone, email, and tax/VAT num
bers (used on Invoices/Reports). * User Management: Create/Edit users
(Admin, Manager, Cashier, Accountant). * Roles & Permissions: Define
what each role can do (e.g., Cashier can sell but not edit stock). * Audit Log:
Track who did what (login times, critical edits, deletions).
2. Inventory Management
Purpose: Track stock levels, product costs, and movements. * Product Man
agement: Add/Edit products with images, barcodes, SKUs, buy price, sell
price, and tax rates. * Categories & Brands: Organize products for better
reporting. * Stock Tracking: Real-time visibility of quantity in hand. * Low
Stock Alerts: Dashboard warnings when items fall below a set threshold. *
Stock Adjustment: Manually correct stock for damages or losses. * Barcode
Printing: Generate and print barcode labels for products.
3. Purchase Module (Procurement)
Purpose: Manage buying of goods and vendor relationships. * Vendor Man
agement: Save supplier details and payment terms. * Purchase Orders
(PO): Create orders to send to suppliers. * Goods Received Note (GRN):
Record stock when it physically arrives (updates Inventory). * Purchase
Bill/Invoice: Enter the bill amount and due date. * Purchase Return:
Manage returns to suppliers for faulty goods (decreases Inventory).
4. Sales Module (POS & Invoicing)
Purpose: Process customer sales and revenue. * Point of Sale (POS): Fast
interface for retail counters (Barcode scan, Quick add). * Invoicing: Profes
sional A4/Letter invoice generation for B2B. * Quotations/Estimates: Send
price estimates to clients (can be converted to Invoice). * Sales Return:
Handle customer returns and refunds (updates Inventory). * Discount Man
agement: Apply line-item or total bill discounts. * Thermal & Standard
Printing: Support for receipt printers and standard laser printers.
1
5. Customer & CRM
Purpose: Manage customer data and debts. * Customer Database: Save
names, numbers, addresses. * Credit Limit: Set maximum credit allowed for a
customer. * Customer Ledger: View transaction history (Sales vs. Payments).
* Receivables: Report showing who owes money and how much (Aging report).
6. Accounting & Ledger
Purpose: Double-entry bookkeeping to track financial health. * Chart of
Accounts: Manage Assets, Liabilities, Equity, Income, and Expenses cate
gories. * Journal Entries: Manual debit/credit entries for adjustments. *
Expense Tracking: Record daily shops expenses (Rent, Electricity, Tea, etc.).
* General Ledger: Detailed view of all transactions for any account. * Trial
Balance: Summary of all ledger balances to ensure books are balanced.
7. Reports & Analytics
Purpose: Business intelligence for decision making. * Sales Reports: Daily,
Weekly, Monthly sales; Best-selling items; Profit per item. * Purchase Re
ports: Purchase history by vendor or item. * Inventory Reports: Stock val
uation (how much money is tied up in stock). * Financial Reports: * Profit
&Loss (P&L): Net profit calculation over a period. * Balance Sheet: Snap
shop of financial standing. * Cash Flow: Cash in vs. Cash out. * Tax/VAT
Report: Summary of tax collected vs. tax paid.
8. Backup & Restore
Purpose: Data security. * Local Backup: One-click backup of the database
f
ile to a secure folder/drive. * Auto-Backup: Schedule automatic backups
(e.g., on application close). * Restore: Ability to load a previous database file
in case of system crash.
